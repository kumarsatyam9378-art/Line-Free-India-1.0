# Feature Roadmap

## Overview
This document outlines the feature roadmap for the Line Free India project. It describes key initiatives, timelines, and milestones to guide development and ensure alignment with our goals. We have successfully implemented core features including real-time salon listing, token booking, queue management, photo uploads, real-time notifications, analytics, Google Login, WhatsApp sharing, dark/light mode, and more (see README.md).

The following 50+ features define the future evolution of the platform.

## Proposed Future Features (50+)

### AI & Machine Learning
1. **AI Styling Recommendations:** Suggest hairstyles based on face shape and previous photos.
2. **AI Booking Assistant (Chatbot):** Natural language booking via an integrated chatbot.
3. **Dynamic Pricing Engine:** AI-driven surge or discount pricing based on real-time demand.
4. **Predictive Churn Analysis:** Identify customers likely to stop visiting and automatically send targeted offers.
5. **Smart Wait Time Estimation:** Advanced ML model for highly accurate wait times based on barber speed history.
6. **Voice-Activated Booking:** Integration with Alexa/Google Assistant for booking appointments.
7. **Automated Review Moderation:** AI to filter spam and highlight actionable feedback.

### Advanced Booking & Queue Management
8. **Multi-Service Booking:** Book hair, beard, and massage in a single seamless flow.
9. **Group Bookings:** Book contiguous slots for families or wedding parties.
10. **Waitlist Auto-Promotion:** Automatically offer a cancelled slot to the next person on the waitlist.
11. **Walk-in Kiosk Mode:** Dedicated UI for tablets in the salon for walk-ins to join the queue.
12. **Recurring Appointments:** Set up weekly or monthly standing appointments.
13. **VIP Fast Track:** Premium option to skip the queue for an extra fee.
14. **Specific Chair Selection:** Book not just the service, but a specific physical chair.
15. **Service Duration Customization:** Barbers can adjust expected duration for specific clients based on hair type.

### Payments & Financials
16. **Integrated POS System:** Full Point-of-Sale features for in-store checkout.
17. **Wallet System:** In-app wallet for customers to load funds and get bonus credits.
18. **Split Payments:** Allow customers to split the bill with friends.
19. **Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL):** Integration with Lazypay, Simpl, etc.
20. **Subscription Plans (Salon):** Tiered SaaS plans for businesses (Basic, Pro, Enterprise).
21. **Subscription Plans (Customer):** "Unlimited haircuts" monthly subscriptions.
22. **Automated Tax Calculation:** Real-time GST calculation and reporting for salon owners.
23. **Digital Tipping:** Easy post-service tipping via UPI.

### Social & Community
24. **Salon Feed:** Instagram-like feed for salons to post before/after photos.
25. **Customer Lookbook:** Personal gallery of past haircuts to show the barber.
26. **Community Forums:** Discuss hair care tips and product recommendations.
27. **Influencer Integration:** Special badges and referral tracking for local influencers.
28. **In-App Stories:** 24-hour disappearing updates from favorite salons.
29. **Gift Cards:** Purchase and send digital gift cards to friends.

### Loyalty & Retention
30. **Gamified Loyalty Tiers:** Bronze, Silver, Gold tiers with unlockable perks.
31. **Birthday Rewards:** Automated special offers sent on the customer's birthday.
32. **Stamp Card Digitization:** Digital version of "buy 10 get 1 free".
33. **Cashback Rewards:** Earn a percentage back on every visit to spend on the next.

### Staff & B2B Management
34. **Staff Performance Dashboard:** Track individual barber revenue, speed, and ratings.
35. **Payroll Management:** Calculate commission and base pay for staff.
36. **Shift Scheduling:** Roster management for staff with leave requests.
37. **Inventory Management:** Track hair products, shampoos, and automatic reordering alerts.
38. **Multi-Branch Support:** Manage multiple salon locations from a single admin account.
39. **B2B Supply Marketplace:** Order supplies directly from wholesalers within the app.

### E-Commerce & Retail
40. **Product Sales:** Salons can sell hair gel, shampoo, etc., directly through the app.
41. **Home Delivery:** Partner with delivery services for hair products.
42. **Product Recommendations:** Automated suggestions based on the service received.

### Platform & Technical
43. **Progressive Web App (PWA):** Installable web app for offline access.
44. **Native iOS & Android Apps:** Full React Native or Flutter rebuild for app stores.
45. **Multi-Language Support:** Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, etc.
46. **Offline Mode for Barbers:** Continue managing the queue during internet outages, syncing when back online.
47. **Apple Watch / Wear OS App:** View queue status from a smartwatch.
48. **API Access for Salons:** Open API for large chains to integrate with their existing CRMs.

### Marketing & CRM
49. **SMS/WhatsApp Marketing Campaigns:** Send bulk promotional messages.
50. **Custom App Branding:** White-label option for large salon chains to have their own branded app version.
51. **Customer Segmentation:** Filter customers by "New", "Regular", "Slipping Away" for targeted campaigns.
52. **Automated Feedback Loop:** Triggered surveys sent 2 hours after the appointment.

## Milestones
- **Q3 2026:** Launch AI Recommendations and Advanced Queue Management.
- **Q4 2026:** Roll out comprehensive B2B Management & Inventory tools.
- **Q1 2027:** Introduce POS System and Customer Wallet integration.
- **Q2 2027:** Expand to Native Mobile Apps and Multi-Language support.

## Conclusion
This roadmap will be updated regularly to reflect progress, market feedback, and changes in priorities.
